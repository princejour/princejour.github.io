Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how My Teacher (the “App”), package name com.walhero.myteacher, handles information when you use the App.

1. School communication information

The App is designed for communication between teachers and families. It may display student names, class information, family access codes, and messages entered by a teacher.

In the current version, this information is processed locally inside the App. The App does not currently upload teacher messages, family codes, student names, or teacher passcodes to a server operated by the developer. Current in-app demo state may be removed when the App is closed, its data is cleared, or it is uninstalled.

Users should not enter unnecessary sensitive personal information. If a future version introduces Firebase, cloud synchronization, accounts, or another server-based feature, this policy and the store Data Safety declaration must be updated before that version is released.

2. Advertising and third-party services

The App uses Google AdMob to provide rewarded advertisements after the monthly free-message limit. A locked message is unlocked only when the advertising provider confirms that the rewarded ad was completed.

Depending on your device, region, settings, and consent choices, Google and its advertising partners may process information such as:

  • IP address and approximate location derived from it;
  • device and advertising identifiers where available;
  • advertising interactions and app activity related to ad delivery;
  • diagnostic, performance, security, and fraud-prevention information.

Google processes this information under its own policies. See the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information for advertising.

3. Consent and privacy choices

Where required by law, including in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, a Google-certified consent message may be shown before advertising requests are made. The App checks the consent status at launch and does not request an ad until Google’s consent system indicates that advertising requests are allowed.

When required, the App provides a visible “Privacy choices” option that lets you review or change advertising consent. The App also provides direct access to this Privacy Policy.

4. Permissions and network access

The App uses internet and network-state access to load Google AdMob content and related consent messages. The current version does not request contacts, microphone, camera, precise location, SMS, or phone permissions.

5. Data retention and deletion

Locally processed App state can be removed by clearing the App’s storage or uninstalling the App. Information processed independently by Google AdMob is retained and protected according to Google’s policies and your applicable consent choices.

6. Children and family use

The App is intended to be operated by teachers, parents, and guardians for school communication. It is not intended for children to independently create an advertising account or submit personal information. Adults responsible for using the App should avoid entering information that is not necessary for school communication.

7. Security

Family access codes and teacher passcodes are intended to reduce casual unauthorized access, but users must keep those codes confidential. No mobile application or data transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. International processing

Advertising providers may process information on servers located outside your country. Where applicable, those providers are responsible for using appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

9. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the App, its storage model, advertising features, or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when material changes are made.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the developer through the official princejour GitHub profile or the developer contact details shown on the App’s official store listing.
