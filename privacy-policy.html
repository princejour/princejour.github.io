Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Walhero Live Wallpaper (the “App”), package name com.walhero.livewallpaper, handles information when you use the App.

1. Information handled by the App

The App does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or precise location.

  • User-selected videos: The App accesses a video only after you select it through the Android system file picker. The selected video is copied to the App’s private local storage so it can be used as a live wallpaper.
  • App preferences: Settings such as the wallpaper sound preference are stored locally on your device.

The developer does not operate a server that receives your selected videos. Video content is not uploaded by the App to the developer.

2. Advertising and third-party services

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Depending on your device, region, settings, and consent choices, Google and its partners may process information such as:

  • IP address and approximate location derived from it;
  • device and advertising identifiers where available;
  • advertising interactions and app activity related to ad delivery;
  • diagnostic, performance, and fraud-prevention information.

Google processes this information under its own policies. See the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information for advertising.

3. Consent and privacy choices

Where required by law, including in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, a Google-certified consent message may be shown before personalized advertising is requested. Your choices determine whether personalized, non-personalized, limited, or other eligible ad serving modes may be used.

You may also reset or limit your device advertising identifier through your Android privacy settings. Clearing the App’s data or uninstalling it removes locally stored App preferences and the App’s private copy of the selected video.

4. Permissions

The App may request storage access on older Android versions so that you can choose a local video. On supported Android versions, the system document picker is used, and you control which file is made available to the App.

5. Data retention and security

The App keeps the selected video and preferences locally until you clear them, clear the App’s data, or uninstall the App. Information processed by Google AdMob is retained and protected according to Google’s policies and your applicable consent choices.

6. Children’s privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly request personal information directly from children. Parents or guardians can contact the developer if they believe there is a privacy concern involving a child.

7. International processing

Advertising providers may process information on servers located outside your country. Where applicable, those providers are responsible for using appropriate legal safeguards for international transfers.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the App, its advertising features, or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when material changes are made.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the developer through the official princejour GitHub profile or the developer contact details shown on the App’s official store listing.
